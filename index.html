How to ferment a cucumber

Ingredients

  • Cucumber 1.2kg
  • Onion 150gr
  • Round black pepper 1 teaspoon
  • Parsley 3-4 branches
  • Chili 1 count
  • Salt 125gr
  • Sugar 1 teaspoon
  • Vinegar 250ml (5%)

Process

  1. Put 75gr of salt into 1 liter of water.
    Put your cucumbers in the brine and let it sit for 12 hours before getting it out and drying it.
  2. Prepare your chili, parsley and onion. Put your cucumbers and spices into a sterilizated glass
  3. Boil a liter of water, 50gr of salt and sugar and pick bubbles up. Add vinegar at the end.
  4. Put them all up into the jar and boil them with lid on for 30 minutes before letting it sit for 3-4 days.
